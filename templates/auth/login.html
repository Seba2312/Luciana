Why do we use Google Sign‑In?

Google Sign‑In verifies your identity and grants Luciana permission to create and update events in your Google Calendar.

We only access your basic profile and calendar data for this purpose and never share it with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

What is Luciana?

Luciana helps apartment owners and housing associations organise meetings, share agendas and keep track of attendees in one place.

Anything you plan here is automatically synced to Google Calendar so everyone stays informed.
